### Title
Temporary Power Disruption on 26AUG2024

### Description
On 26AUG2024 the incoming power supply to the Mark Cuban Cost Plus Manufacturing and Compounding Facility experienced a power disruption at ~0800 am. The Facility Backup power supply activated as intended restoring power in less than one minute. At the time of disruption no batch manufacturing operations were in progress. As the duration was brief, no commercial manufacturing activities were in progress, and the backup generator was activated as planned the initial categorization is minor.  BMS and EMS trends will confirm exact time and duration and also provide the impact.
